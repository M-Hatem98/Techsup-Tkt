Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to operate and improve the Techsup Tkt platform, including:

  • Account details (company name, email, phone)
  • Ticket data, messages, and activity logs
  • Billing and subscription information
  • Technical data such as IP address and browser type

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to provide system functionality, manage subscriptions, enhance performance, and maintain security.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Information is shared only with trusted service providers such as payment gateways and hosting services.

4. Security

We apply industry-standard security measures including encryption, access control, and continuous monitoring.

5. Cookies

Cookies help us improve usability and maintain secure sessions. You may disable cookies in your browser settings.

6. Your Rights

You can access, update, or request deletion of your data at any time, subject to legal obligations.

7. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated periodically. Changes will be published on this page.

8. Contact
